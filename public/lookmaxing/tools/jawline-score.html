What makes a strong jawline

A defined jawline reads from the gonial angle (the corner of the jaw), the taper from cheekbone to chin, and left-right symmetry. We compute all three and combine them into a single score.

Much of how a jaw reads is fluid, posture and body composition — all modifiable. The Bespoke Aesthetic Blueprint gives you the 90-day protocol to sharpen it.
