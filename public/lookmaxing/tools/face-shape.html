How the face shape detector works

Your face shape comes from the ratios between face length, cheekbone width, jaw width and forehead width. We measure all four from 478 facial landmarks and classify the closest shape, with a confidence score.

Knowing your shape tells you which haircuts, beards and frames balance your proportions. For the full styling and grooming protocol tailored to your face, see the Bespoke Aesthetic Blueprint.
