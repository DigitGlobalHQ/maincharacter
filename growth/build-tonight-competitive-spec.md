# Lookmaxxing — Build-Tonight Competitive Spec

> Pillar: Lookmaxxing (web PWA, ₹1,499/mo, "The Consultant" voice)
> Date: 28 May 2026
> Author: growth-research-agent
> Purpose: ground tonight's feature-complete build (B1–B5) in evidence about the actual competitive surface, so the build teams know exactly what to attack and what to ignore.

---

## Executive answer (3 bullets)

- The category is dominated by US-priced ($3.99–$14.99/wk), App-Store-native, hype-coded face-rating apps that monetise the audit moment hard and have no real Day-2 surface. Their cohort dies inside 7 days because the score never changes for them, and "daily routine" is a static checklist nobody re-opens. Source pattern across Umax, LooksMax AI, Maxxing, LooxUP: lengthy onboarding → score-reveal → paywall → static plan → silence.
- The single defensible wedge for MainCharacter is **measurement-over-time rendered as a side-by-side mirror artifact**, not as a number that goes up. Every competitor sells "your 10/10 look" (Maxxing) or "your potential" (LooksMax AI) as an AI-generated fantasy image. MainCharacter can sell **your actual face on Day 30 next to your actual face on Day 1**, captioned in The Consultant's voice. None of them ship this credibly today.
- QOVES is the outlier — clinical, science-cited, one-shot $150 PDF, no daily loop, no app. They own "trust" in this category by default because nobody else even competes for it. MainCharacter's anti-hype posture lets us take the daily-loop version of that trust position — clinical evidence rendered as a habit, not a 23-page PDF.

## Per-competitor research

### 1. LooksMax AI
- **Identity:** AI face-rater with hairstyle simulator and "daily coaching"; multiple iOS SKUs under similar names (Mnkybrain Labs is one publisher). Note: "LooksMax AI" resolves to at least 4 distinct App Store listings — I treated the most-cited Mnkybrain build as canonical.
- **Price:** Free tier (basic score). Pro $9.99/wk. Premium ~$19.99/mo. Lifetime ~$99.99. ANALYZER plan $14.99/mo with Quest System + XP & Levels + Streak Rewards. [aichief.com](https://aichief.com/ai-lifestyle-tools/looksmax-ai/), [opentools.ai](https://opentools.ai/tools/looksmax-ai)
- **Free hook:** Basic LooksMax Score + general advice. Onboarding kicks off with a carousel of sample AI-generated ratings so the user sees the format before paying. [screensdesign.com](https://screensdesign.com/showcase/looksmax-ai)
- **Paid promise:** Personalised plan across hair/skin/style; AI hairstyle simulator; re-upload-to-see-score-move loop.
- **UX standout:** Streak calendar view + Quest System gamification layer over face-rating. Score *updates* as you re-upload — the come-back hook is "did my number move?" [hyscaler.com](https://hyscaler.com/insights/looksmax-ai-self-improvement-app-men/)
- **Voice / vibe:** Bro-coded, gamified, RPG-tinged (XP, Levels, Quests).
- **Trust signals:** Thin. App Store reviews + influencer carousel. No clinical citations.
- **Daily loop:** Streak + daily coaching checklist + occasional re-scan. Whether users actually return is unclear; the streak UI exists but I could not verify D7/D30 retention.
- **Retention failure mode (best guess):** The score is the entire product. Once it stops moving (which happens fast because facial photography varies more day-to-day than actual face does), the user concludes the AI is noise and leaves.

### 2. Umax — Become Hot
- **Identity:** The category leader by downloads. AI face rater with masculinity / jawline / cheekbone scoring. Madden-card-style score reveal. iOS-led, founder publicly cited $500K/mo revenue at the 23-yr-old founder mark. [Fortune via Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/looksmaxxing-apps-rate-teen-boys-163942148.html), [Techmeme](https://www.techmeme.com/240701/p21)
- **Price:** $3.99/wk. No free trial. One free scan; subsequent scans paywalled OR unlock by inviting 3 friends (viral loop disguised as a paywall). [justuseapp.com](https://justuseapp.com/en/app/6471026798/umax-maximize-your-looks/reviews), [paywallpro.app](https://www.paywallpro.app/share/Umax---Become-Hot-1.4.2-us?id=521)
- **Free hook:** Exactly one scan, then forced choice — pay or share. [Google Play complaint thread](https://support.google.com/googleplay/thread/311514006/)
- **Paid promise:** Unlimited scans + detailed breakdown.
- **UX standout:** Three-photo sequential upload (front/side/extra) that builds anticipation; Madden-rating-card reveal animation; share-3-friends viral unlock. [Superwall analysis](https://superwall.com/blog/part-2-how-to-design-a-viral-app-in-2025/), [Adapty paywall library](https://adapty.io/paywall-library/umax-become-hot/)
- **Voice / vibe:** Bro-coded, sports-card energy, "ultimate bro in navigating the looksmaxing journey." Maximally hype.
- **Trust signals:** Download count is the trust signal (7M+ per Fortune). No clinical posture. Heavy negative press: psychologists publicly call it part of a teen mental-health crisis. [Fortune via Yahoo](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/looksmaxxing-apps-rate-teen-boys-163942148.html), [The Star](https://www.thestar.com.my/tech/tech-news/2024/07/03/looksmaxxing-is-exacerbating-the-youth-mental-health-crisis-psychologists-say)
- **Daily loop:** Effectively none. Users complain you can only scan once/week unless you pay; even paid users have nothing to come back to after the score reveal. [justuseapp reviews](https://justuseapp.com/en/app/6471026798/umax-maximize-your-looks/reviews)
- **Retention failure mode:** No second act. Score → paywall → silence. App Store reviews cite refund-denied, infinite-loop processing screens, crashes. Pure top-of-funnel monetisation.

### 3. QOVES
- **Identity:** Premium one-shot facial-analysis PDF service from a UK-origin team; positions on academic citations and clinical scales (Fitzpatrick, Bauman, Goldman, Lancer). Hosts a podcast and a long-form insights blog. [qoves.com](https://www.qoves.com/), [futurepedia.io](https://www.futurepedia.io/tool/qoves)
- **Price:** Free basic facial-assessment tool. Reports at $100 / $150 / $250. Expedited delivery +$80. Haircut recommendation +$25. [Pricing summary aggregator](https://www.pineapplebuilder.com/ai-tools/qoves)
- **Free hook:** Basic AI facial-assessment tool on the website. Most of the funnel is content-led — Spotify podcast "QOVES Glow Up," long insights articles.
- **Paid promise:** 23-page report citing 2000+ academic studies and 112 parameters; "functional face plan" with non-surgical recommendations. [futurepedia.io](https://www.futurepedia.io/tool/qoves), [Medium honest review](https://alessaweiler.medium.com/my-honest-qoves-review-i-tried-their-aesthetic-analysis-these-are-my-results-be7aa6d9b61f)
- **UX standout:** The PDF itself — long-form, citation-heavy, clinically illustrated. Reads like a derm consult write-up.
- **Voice / vibe:** Clinical, restrained, citation-forward. Closest to MainCharacter's posture of any competitor researched. Tagline: "Realistic & objective beauty advice to achieve your glow up without surgery."
- **Trust signals:** Academic citations, ethnicity-aware scales, named clinical frameworks, no fake celebrity testimonials.
- **Daily loop:** None. It is a one-shot artifact, not a habit.
- **Retention failure mode:** No second purchase. Reviewer in [Medium piece](https://alessaweiler.medium.com/my-honest-qoves-review-i-tried-their-aesthetic-analysis-these-are-my-results-be7aa6d9b61f) flags "information overload, hard to process everything." High-trust front door, no back door. Users read it once and move on.

### 4. Maxxing — LooksMaxxing Glow Up
- **Identity:** AI glow-up coach with face score + daily routine + try-on for hair and clothing. Flutter-built; iOS + Android. [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/maxxing-looksmaxxing-glow-up/id6502996574), [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.glowup.maxxing.maxxing_flutter)
- **Price:** Subscription-heavy with multiple weekly tiers and one-off paid features; specific INR/USD pricing varied in research and I could not confirm a single canonical figure. App Store reviews complain about billing surprises and feature delta between web and app. [App Store listing reviews]
- **Free hook:** Onboarding selfie + score reveal; paywall hit early per user reports.
- **Paid promise:** Custom glow-up routine, mewing/jawline workouts, skincare cleanser/toner steps, haircut visualisation, "see your 10/10 look revealed." [Maxxing.app](https://maxxing.app/)
- **UX standout:** AI-generated "10/10 you" image as the carrot; visual try-on for hairstyles and outfits. The reveal is **fantasy-you**, not progress-you.
- **Voice / vibe:** Polished hype. Less bro than Umax, more aspirational/consumer-beauty.
- **Trust signals:** Standard App Store star ratings; no clinical citations.
- **Daily loop:** Daily to-do checklist of skincare + mewing tasks. Tracks completion. Whether the visualisation refreshes daily is not visible from public surface.
- **Retention failure mode (best guess):** Fantasy image creates expectation-versus-reality gap. User does the daily checklist for a week, looks in the mirror, sees themselves (not the 10/10 render), and concludes the app lied. The reveal mechanic is the trap.

### 5. LooxUP (LooksMax Face Rating AI)
- **Identity:** iOS face-rater + improvement-plan app, published under "LooksMax Face Rating AI — LooxUP." Lifestyle-app category. [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/looksmax-face-rating-ai-looxup/id6478697267), [looxup.app](https://looxup.app/)
- **Price:** 3-day free trial on weekly plan; $10/wk after trial. [App Store listing referenced via overchat.ai](https://overchat.ai/ai-hub/best-looksmaxing-ai-tools)
- **Free hook:** 3-day trial gates the entire product; no free tier.
- **Paid promise:** 50+ facial parameters analysed; score 1–10; plans for jawline / skin / hair; "Avatars" feature generating AI portraits of the user across hairstyles and emotions.
- **UX standout:** Avatar generator — user sees AI-rendered versions of themselves with different hair and expressions.
- **Voice / vibe:** Hype-coded, monetisation-aggressive per user feedback.
- **Trust signals:** Thin. Mixed reviews; users report identical advice regardless of which photo is uploaded ("complete BS"). [overchat.ai review summary](https://overchat.ai/ai-hub/best-looksmaxing-ai-tools)
- **Daily loop:** Personalised routine claimed, but reviews suggest content is generic and doesn't dynamically change. Could not verify a true daily-return mechanic.
- **Retention failure mode:** Trial-end paywall + generic content = high D7 churn. Same advice for every photo erodes credibility within the first week.

## Anti-hype check — tactics we will NOT copy

Past sub-agent briefs have at times drifted toward these. Reaffirming the line tonight:

- **Urgency timers** ("offer expires in 14:59") — Umax-style. We do not use countdowns on the paywall.
- **Madden-card score-reveal animation** — Umax's signature. We do not stage a reveal moment around a single number.
- **Fantasy "10/10 you" AI render** — Maxxing's hook. We do not show users a face that is not theirs.
- **Streak-broken punishment graphics** — LooksMax AI Quest System. Missing a day cannot feel like failure in our surface.
- **Celebrity-comparison features** — looksmax.org-adjacent ("you score 6.2; Chris Hemsworth is 8.4"). Never.
- **Share-3-friends-to-unlock viral gate** — Umax's growth loop. We do not gate results behind social coercion.
- **Hidden weekly pricing that hides the annual cost** — every competitor does this. We show monthly INR plainly.
- **Body-shaming triggers in ad creative** — would also violate Meta policy. We do not frame "before" as ugly.
- **Medical/derm claims we cannot substantiate** — also Meta policy + DPDPA risk.
- **Gamified XP/Quests/Levels grafted onto self-image** — LooksMax AI ANALYZER plan. The Mirror Level system is intentionally *not* an XP grind.

## What I'm uncertain about / what I'd need to learn next

- **Actual D7/D30 retention numbers for Umax, LooksMax AI, Maxxing.** Public claims are revenue ($500K/mo for Umax) but not cohort retention. Would need data.ai / Sensor Tower paid pull. Without it, "their cohort dies" is an informed inference from review patterns, not a verified number. Flagged as estimate.
- **Whether any competitor has launched a true Day-30 side-by-side reveal recently.** Maxx Report surfaced in search ([Google Play listing](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.looksmaxxreport.app)) with "AI-generated before/after images" — could not verify whether these are real user photos or generated. If real, that's the only competitor with our reveal mechanic and we should look closer.
- **India-specific availability + pricing in INR for each.** App Store regional pricing for India was not retrievable in this pass. Worth a manual founder-side check on an Indian iPhone to confirm Umax / LooksMax AI INR price points before tonight's pricing-comparison copy ships.
- **Whether QOVES has shipped any subscription product since their podcast launch.** Their entire moat is one-shot — if they convert to a recurring model in the next quarter, the trust-position fight gets harder.
- **Indian competitors specifically.** None of the five names provided are India-origin. Worth a follow-up brief on whether Plix / Tata 1mg / Indian derm subscription startups have any aesthetic-tracking surface launching in 2026.
