How facial symmetry is scored

We find your facial midline and compare matching points on the left and right — eyes, nose, mouth and jaw — for how evenly they sit. Each region is scored, then combined into an overall figure out of 100.

Perfect symmetry is rare and not the goal. The Bespoke Aesthetic Blueprint focuses on the modifiable signals — fluid, posture, grooming — that move the read.
