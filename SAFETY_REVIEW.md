## 1. What was wrong (the liability)

The live Lookmaxxing protocol was generating **medical and pharmacological advice**
with no disclaimer and no regulatory cover. Concretely, these shipped to users:

- `Minoxidil 5%, once daily`, `Ketoconazole 2% shampoo`, `Microneedling 0.5–1mm weekly`
- `Discuss finasteride with a dermatologist`, `Minoxidil + finasteride to preserve…`
- `Start a low-strength retinoid at night`, `DO NOT mix retinoid with niacinamide`
- `DO NOT take biotin without a deficiency`, `collagen supplement`
- `Protein at every meal`, `crash diet`, `drop water weight` framing
- `RCT-SUPPORTED` / `MECHANISM-SUPPORTED` / `Observational` evidence-tier tags implying
  clinical authority we do not hold.

These lived in the **deterministic** content library (`data/lookmax-content.js` →
`services/protocol.js`) and the hair service (`services/hair.js`), plus there was **no
server-side validator** catching anything the AI itself might emit.

## 2. What we built (the fix)

### a) Central server-side validator — `lib/safety-validator.js`
A single "safe brain" both engines pass through. It **rejects** any task/prose containing:
drug names (minoxidil, finasteride, ketoconazole, tretinoin/retinoid, dutasteride,
spironolactone, hydroquinone, salicylic/glycolic/azelaic/benzoyl, AHA/BHA…),
supplement names (biotin, collagen, creatine, ashwagandha, "supplement"…),
procedures (microneedling, dermaroller, transplant/FUE/PRP, botox/fillers, surgery, laser),
**dosages/strengths** (`5%`, `2.5 mg`, `0.5mm`…), **evidence framing** (RCT, clinical,
study, placebo, evidence-tier, mechanism-supported), and **prescriptive diet** (protein at
every meal, calorie deficit, crash diet, water weight, fasting, keto). Forbidden tasks are
**replaced** with a deterministic task from a built-in safe-task library
(sleep, hydration, generic sun protection, gentle cleanse/moisturise, posture, grooming/
beard geometry, haircut & style, wardrobe colour). Forbidden prose is replaced with the
canonical fallback: **"This is one for a qualified professional."**

### b) Allow-list-only content
`data/lookmax-content.js` (PROTOCOL_LIBRARY) and `services/hair.js` were **rewritten** to
the allow-list only — no drugs, no dosages, no evidence tiers, honest referral to a
qualified professional when recession is a real concern.

### c) Wired into every serving path
- `services/protocol.js` — daily checklist passes `sanitizeProtocolDay()` before serving.
- `services/hair.js` — Norwood guidance passes `sanitizeProtocolDay()` before serving.
- `services/vision.js` — legacy audit diagnosis passes a prose tripwire; falls back if unsafe.
- `routes/lookmaxing.js` — the live audit report is deep-walked by `_sanitizeReport()`; any
  unsafe string field anywhere in the report is replaced with the safe fallback.

### d) RCT/MECHANISM tags removed entirely
Removed from the data, from `services/protocol.js` output, and from the rendered UI
(`public/lookmax/protocol.html`, `public/lookmax/hair.html`).

### e) Context-vs-quest (user-metrices.docx)
The live audit engine (`data/lookmaxing-audit-prompts.js`) already enforces this:
unchangeable traits (bone structure, canthal tilt, symmetry, harmony, nose/lip/forehead,
undertone, contrast, archetype, native hair density) are **context only — never scored,
never tasked**. The validator exposes `isContextOnly()` / `CONTEXT_ONLY_TRAITS` as a shared
guard, and only changeable metrics (skin, hairline/beard geometry, haircut match, hair
condition, under-eye, posture, wardrobe cohesion) earn a score + a safe task.

## 3. Validator test results

`tests/safety-validator.test.js` — **27 tests pass.** Asserts the validator REJECTS:
minoxidil, finasteride, ketoconazole, retinoid, biotin, `5%`, `2.5 mg`, `RCT-supported`,
`protein at every meal`, microneedling `0.5-1mm`, crash diet / water weight, collagen
supplement — and ACCEPTS: sleep, hydration, sunscreen, posture, gentle cleanse, salt/alcohol
reduction, a hairline-appropriate cut. Plus: every safe-library entry is self-safe (no
infinite-replace loop), context-vs-quest classification, `sanitizeProtocolDay`, and the prose
tripwire.

`tests/audit-report-safety.test.js` — **2 tests pass.** A deliberately-poisoned model report
(minoxidil in firstImpression, ketoconazole in a fix, microneedling/biotin in quests/plan) is
fully scrubbed; safe fields survive untouched.

`tests/lookmax-hair.test.js` — rewritten: every Norwood stage now emits **zero** medical
content and source copy never false-trips the validator.

**Full suite: 1165 passed, 0 failed** after the change.
