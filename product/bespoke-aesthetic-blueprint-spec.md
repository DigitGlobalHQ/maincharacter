Methodology, Safety & Limitations
This is a photographic aesthetic and image-consulting assessment, not a medical or dermatological diagnosis. Scores derive from a single submitted capture under non-clinical conditions and are directional guidance for grooming, styling, posture, and skincare.
Items marked ℞ (e.g. tretinoin) are prescription-only medicines. They are listed as directives to discuss with a licensed dermatologist, who must set the molecule, strength, and frequency for your skin. Do not self-source or self-prescribe. Patch-test all new topicals; introduce one active at a time; SPF is mandatory alongside any retinoid.
Fixed osseous metrics (gonial angle, canthal tilt) are documented as strategic context only and are excluded from the protocol and the projected ceiling. No directive in this document targets bone structure. All recommended habits are health-positive; discontinue anything that causes pain or irritation and consult a professional.
