What is canthal tilt?

Canthal tilt is the angle between the inner and outer corners of your eye. A slight positive (upturned) tilt is widely associated with an alert, attractive eye read; neutral is balanced; negative reads tired or downturned.

We measure both eyes and average them. Brow grooming, under-eye care and rest change how the tilt reads — the Bespoke Aesthetic Blueprint covers the periorbital protocol in full.
